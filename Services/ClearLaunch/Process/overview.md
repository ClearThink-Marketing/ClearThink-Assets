# ClearLaunch Process — The 7-Step Delivery Methodology

This folder holds the step-by-step delivery methodology for ClearLaunch. Each step has its own folder containing the production skill, templates, and a step-specific `overview.md`.

For the full ClearLaunch methodology — pricing, tooling, infrastructure, agent automation, data flow — see [`../ClearLaunch_GTM_Strategy_Blueprint.md`](../ClearLaunch_GTM_Strategy_Blueprint.md).

## Timeline — 4 phases across 8 weeks

| Phase | Weeks | Steps |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding & Discovery | 1–2 | [Step 1 — Onboarding](./1-Onboarding/) |

## The 7 steps

| Step | Produces | Folder |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Onboarding** | Client Portal (infrastructure; no client-facing deliverable) | [`1-Onboarding/`](./1-Onboarding/) |
| **2 — ICP** | ICP document + summary deck (B2B or B2C) | [`2-ICP/`](./2-ICP/) |
| **3 — Market Research** | Market landscape document + summary deck (B2B or B2C) | [`3-Market-Research/`](./3-Market-Research/) |
| **4 — UVP** | UVP document + summary deck | [`4-UVP/`](./4-UVP/) |
| **5 — Offer Dev** | Offer Development document + summary deck (3-tier ladder) | [`5-Offer-Dev/`](./5-Offer-Dev/) |
| **6 — Channel Strategy** | Channel strategy document + summary deck + projections workbook (two-phase) | [`6-Channel-Strategy/`](./6-Channel-Strategy/) |
| **7 — Metrics & Roadmap** | KPI framework + 90-day implementation plan | *not yet built* |

---

## How the steps connect

Each step's output becomes the next step's input.

- Steps 1 → 2 → 3 build the factual foundation — portal, audience, market
- Steps 4 → 5 build messaging and monetization on top of that foundation
- Step 6 translates messaging and offers into where to show up and what to expect
- Step 7 turns the whole strategy into measurable action

A weak output at any step compounds downstream. The Blueprint expands on why strategy-first sequencing matters here.
