# ClearThink Assets

This is the source-of-truth repo for **ClearThink Marketing** — company positioning, service processes, workshop templates, production skills, and operational playbooks.

It's built to be read by two audiences: humans on the team, and the Claude agents that execute ClearThink's service workflows.

---

## About ClearThink

ClearThink is a go-to-market agency that cuts through the noise and gives small business owners a clear plan for growth.

I started ClearThink because the marketing landscape has changed — AI is reshaping how customers discover brands, tactics multiply faster than most owners can evaluate them, and too many agencies keep selling services without a strategy behind them. Small business owners end up with a pile of deliverables and no clear plan.

ClearThink was built for the owners who refuse to settle for that. The goal is to amplify impact-driven small businesses — giving them a clear path to growth so they can spend less time second-guessing marketing and more time building what they're here to build.

**The operating principle:** diagnose before you prescribe.

For the full company overview — services, pricing, who we serve, team — see **[`Overview.md`](./Overview.md)**.
