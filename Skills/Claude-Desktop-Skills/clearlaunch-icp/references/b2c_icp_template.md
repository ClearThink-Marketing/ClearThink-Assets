**Relationship to Buyer Personas:**

This ICP defines the external targeting criteria. Within this ICP, you will likely have 2-4 buyer personas representing different motivation clusters — people who meet the same criteria but buy for different reasons. The ICP tells your media buyer WHO to target. The personas tell your copywriter WHAT to say.

Next Step: After completing this ICP, develop buyer personas for each distinct motivation cluster.
