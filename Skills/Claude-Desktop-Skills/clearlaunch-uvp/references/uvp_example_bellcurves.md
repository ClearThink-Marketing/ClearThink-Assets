# Completed UVP Example: Bell Curves (Education/Test Prep)

This is an example of a completed UVP workshop for an education services client. Notice how raw workshop answers were refined into specific, tangible differentiators — not generic claims.

## UVP Synthesis (The Key Output)

**Top 3 Differentiators:**

| 1. | Knowledge-First Curriculum: We build the foundation competitors assume exists. Our materials, teacher training, and approach start at an 800 baseline — not 1200. We add the Knowledge layer (remedial modules, fundamental drills) that Princeton Review and Kaplan skip entirely. |
| :---- | :---- |
| **2.** | Score-Band Differentiation: Cohort-based strategies by score range (600/800/1000/1200/1400) with tailored approaches for each band. Not personalization, but smart differentiation — we know what an 800-scoring student needs vs. a 1200-scoring student and plan accordingly. |
| **3.** | Cultural Alignment + Live Delivery: Tutors who are products of the same environments they teach in. In-person instruction in schools when everyone else has gone virtual. We're the outsiders who actually understand the population — not Ivy League grads reading from a script. |

**Draft UVP Statement:**

We help charter networks and public schools move average-scoring students into college-competitive range through curriculum and instruction built for where students actually are — not where competitors assume they should be. Unlike Princeton Review and Kaplan — who focus on high-performers and assume fundamentals are already in place — we start with diagnostics, build knowledge plans that address real gaps, and deliver live instruction with culturally-aligned teachers who connect with underserved student populations.

**Elevator Pitch (30 seconds):**

"Most test prep companies are built for kids already scoring 1200 who want to hit 1400. That's not our student. Our student is at 800 and needs to get to 1000 — a move that takes them from community college to state school, from limited options to real choices. We build curriculum for that student. We diagnose where they actually are, fill the knowledge gaps everyone else ignores, and put teachers in classrooms who come from the same communities they're serving. The result? We've moved entire school averages up 60 points. We've displaced Kaplan at NYC Public Schools."

**Positioning Statement (Internal Use):**

For charter networks and public schools serving average and below-average scoring students
Who need test prep that actually works for their population
Bell Curves is the only test prep provider
That builds curriculum from an 800-baseline assumption, adds the knowledge layer competitors skip, and delivers live instruction with culturally-aligned teachers
Because we've spent 20 years working exclusively with underserved populations and developed proprietary methods proven to move scores in environments where traditional test prep fails.

## Why This UVP Works

1. **Specific competitor callouts**: Names Princeton Review and Kaplan — makes the differentiation concrete
2. **Tangible methodology**: "Knowledge + Content + Strategy" vs competitors' "Content + Strategy only"
3. **Measurable proof**: "60-point average increase", "Displaced Kaplan at NYC Public Schools"
4. **Emotional resonance**: "From community college to state school, from limited options to real choices"
5. **Cultural authenticity**: "Tutors who are products of the same environments they teach in"
