# Channel Strategy & Customer Journey Template

ClearLaunch System | Step 6: Channel Strategy & Customer Journey

*Purpose: This template guides the development of a complete channel strategy and customer journey map. Phase A evaluates all viable marketing channels, scores them against the ICP, recommends a focused channel mix, allocates budget, and projects performance. Phase B (after client confirmation) maps the customer journey across confirmed channels from awareness through purchase.*

*Adaptive note: This framework is industry-agnostic. The 7-channel universe and TOFU/MOFU/BOFU journey stages apply to any business type — services, SaaS, ecommerce, DTC, B2B, B2C. The agent should reference the completed ICP (Step 2), Market Research (Step 3), UVP (Step 4), and Offer Development (Step 5) to ground every recommendation in real data and client context.*

### 3.1 Channel Selection Rationale

*Decision rules applied in order:*

1. **ICP Presence Gate** — Is the target audience actively present on this channel?
2. **Budget Viability Gate** — Can the client afford meaningful presence given minimum effective spend?
3. **Journey Coverage Check** — Does this channel fill a gap in funnel stage coverage?
4. **Data Availability Tiebreaker** — When two channels score equally, prefer the one with better measurement data.
