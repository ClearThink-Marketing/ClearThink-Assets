# ClearThink Marketing — Overview

ClearThink is a go-to-market agency that cuts through the noise and gives small business owners a clear plan for growth.

Based in Atlanta. Serves clients nationally.

---

## Why ClearThink exists

The marketing landscape has changed. AI is reshaping how customers discover brands, tactics multiply faster than most owners can evaluate them, and too many agencies keep selling services without a strategy behind them — leaving small business owners with a pile of deliverables and no clear plan.

ClearThink was built for the owners who refuse to settle for that. The mission is to amplify impact-driven small businesses — giving them a clear path to growth so they can spend less time second-guessing marketing and more time building what they're here to build.

---

## Who ClearThink serves

ClearThink partners with established small businesses that are ready to grow — typically service-based or B2B operations that have been running for two or more years but never built a real go-to-market strategy, or are scaling up and ready to get serious about growth.

**Industry-agnostic** by design, with e-commerce on the horizon.

**The fit filter.** Best-fit clients aren't defined by revenue bracket — they're defined by mindset. They're rooted in purpose, serious about their brand, and decisive. They don't procrastinate, they're open to ideas, and they're looking for a committed partner — not a vendor.

---

## What makes ClearThink different

**Strategy-first sequencing.** Most agencies lead with execution — ads, content, web builds — and retrofit strategy as needed. ClearThink reverses that order. Strategy work (ICP, UVP, market research, offer development, channel selection) always precedes execution. The operating principle is *diagnose before you prescribe*.

**Custom GTM strategy aligned to owner goals.** Every engagement produces a strategy built specifically for that business — their market, their offer, their customer, and the owner's personal and business goals. It is not a templated framework with the client's name swapped in. How the business positions, who it targets, where it competes, what it offers, and through which channels — all of it is derived from that specific client's inputs, not applied from a playbook.

**Operator background.** Terry's prior experience is in data management consulting at Deloitte, supporting Fortune 500 operations work. That background shapes how ClearThink approaches marketing: evidence-based, measurable, system-oriented.

## Services

### ClearLaunch — Go-to-Market Strategy Sprint
**$1,200 flat · 8 weeks · one-time engagement · industry-agnostic**

ClearLaunch is ClearThink's productized go-to-market strategy sprint. A structured 8-week process that replaces guesswork with a data-backed strategic foundation before a single dollar is spent on campaigns.

**The 7-step process** (delivered across four phases):

1. **Onboarding** — Client portal in Notion, intake workshop, access to all working files
2. **Ideal Client Profile (ICP)** — ICP analysis document + summary deck
3. **Market & Competitive Research** — 50-keyword landscape analysis, competitive audit document + summary deck
4. **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)** — Top 3 differentiators, UVP statement, elevator pitch, positioning document + summary deck
5. **Offer Development** — Micro / Macro / Core three-tier offer ladder document
6. **Channel Strategy** — Two-phase channel plan document + summary deck + projections workbook
7. **KPI Framework & 90-Day Roadmap** — 5–10 priority metrics and a tactical implementation plan

**Timeline:**
Weeks 1–2 Onboarding & Discovery → Weeks 3–4 Strategy Development → Weeks 5–6 Channel & Roadmap → Weeks 7–8 Delivery.

ClearLaunch is designed to stand on its own. Clients leave with a full strategy playbook and clear execution plan — ready to run themselves, hand to a team, or continue with ClearThink.
